## 简介

<div>
This song was written for his sister who recently got married. Congrats to the newlyweds, but seriously, how many of us can say "my brother wrote and performed this song for my husband and me at our wedding?" *sigh* :) anyways, his voice is one of a kind, and seeing him perform live is one of those memories you'll never forget because you can genuinely feel his soul in his voice, with him tapping on his boots.
