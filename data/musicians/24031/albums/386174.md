## 简介

<div>
EP中“Streetlight”“The Myth of Us”这两首继承了 Joshua Radin一贯清新自然的民谣风格，“You Got What I Need”则加入了一些Jazz风格，不同与以往的感觉<br><br>
<br><br>
Although billed as an EP, this four-song iTunes release is more like a glorified single for “Streetlight,” an upbeat piece of contemporary pop/rock that makes room for handclaps, banjo, and acoustic guitar. Apart from that leadoff track, Songs Under a Streetlight is mostly a laid-back record, filled with soft-spoken vocals and quiet ruminations on love. “Brand New Day,” originally part of the 2008 album Simple Times, makes an encore appearance, and Radin trots out some new material in the form of “You Got What I Need” and “The Myth of Us.” <br>
