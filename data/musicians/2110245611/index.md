## 档案

<div>
甲雍  生于1982年。四川甘孜州道孚县玉科草原人。12岁时跟随恩师进行系统的佛学学习。1997年在恩师的亲自带领下前往四川德格佐钦五明佛学院学习佛学文化。<br>
2005年毕业于佐钦五明佛学院。<br>
2006年进入北京民族大学学习。<br>
2009年佛乐专辑《心灵的甘露》《菩提道》<br>
2010年佛乐专辑《觉醒》<br>
2014年佛乐专辑《五路财神》<br>
2017年佛乐专辑《哎玛吙》<br>
2016年认证为四川省甘孜州非物质文化遗产“梵音唱诵”者传承人<br>
2017年获得了美国The Akademia Music Awards 最佳唱诵歌曲奖<br>
Brief Introduction of Jia Yong<br>
Jia Yong was born in the Yuke grassland, Daofu County, Ganzi State, Sichuan Province of China in 1982.<br>
He followed his respected master and started the systematic learning of the Buddhism from the age of 12 years.<br>
Guided by his respecting master, Jia Yong studied Buddhist culture at Zuoqin Five Ming Buddhist College in Dege, Sichuan Province from 1997 to 2005.<br>
Jia Yong pursued advanced studies in Beijing University for Nationalities in 2006.<br>
Major works:<br>
The Buddhist music album in 2009 :《The Dew of the Mind》、《Lime Road》<br>
The Buddhist music album in 2010 :《Awakening》<br>
The Buddhist music album in 2014 :《The Five Jambhalas》<br>
Jia Yong was identified as the representative successor of Ganzi State, Sichuan Province Intangible Culture Heritage” Sanskrit Chanting” in 2016.<br>
《Heart Sutra》 in his new album, won the Best Chanting Song of the Akademia Music Awards of America in 2017.
