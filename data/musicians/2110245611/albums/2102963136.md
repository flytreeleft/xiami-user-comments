## 简介

<div>
<p><br>
</p><p><br>
</p><p>甲雍  生于1982年。四川甘孜州道孚县玉科草原人。12岁时跟随恩师进行系统的佛学学习。1997年在恩师的亲自带领下前往四川德格佐钦五明佛学院学习佛学文化。</p><br>
<p>2005年毕业于佐钦五明佛学院。</p><br>
<p>2006年进入北京民族大学学习。 </p><br>
<p>2009年佛乐专辑《心灵的甘露》《菩提道》</p><br>
<p>2010年佛乐专辑《觉醒》</p><br>
<p>2014年佛乐专辑《五路财神》</p><br>
<p>2017年佛乐专辑《哎玛吙》</p><br>
<p>2016年认证为四川省甘孜州非物质文化遗产“梵音唱诵”者传承人</p><br>
<p> 2017年获得了美国The Akademia Music Awards 最佳唱诵歌曲奖 </p><br>
<p> </p><br>
<p>Brief Introduction of Jia Yong</p><br>
<p>Jia Yong was born in the Yuke grassland, Daofu County, Ganzi State, Sichuan Province of China in 1982.</p><br>
<p>He followed his respected master and started the systematic learning of the Buddhism from the age of 12 years.</p><br>
<p>Guided by his respecting master, Jia Yong studied Buddhist culture at Zuoqin Five Ming Buddhist College in Dege, Sichuan Province from 1997 to 2005.</p><br>
<p>Jia Yong pursued advanced studies in Beijing University for Nationalities in 2006.</p><br>
<p> </p><br>
<p>Major works:</p><br>
<p>The Buddhist music album in 2009 :《The Dew of the Mind》、《Lime Road》</p><br>
<p>The Buddhist music album in 2010 :《Awakening》</p><br>
<p>The Buddhist music album in 2014 :《The Five Jambhalas》</p><br>
<p> </p><br>
<p>Jia Yong was identified as the representative successor of Ganzi State, Sichuan Province Intangible Culture Heritage” Sanskrit Chanting” in 2016.</p><br>
<p>《Heart Sutra》 in his new album, won the Best Chanting Song of the Akademia Music Awards of America in 2017.</p><br>
