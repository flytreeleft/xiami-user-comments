## 简介

<div>
Darius "Helios" single Feat Wayne Snow.<br><br>
RELEASE DATE : Single out / Remixes EP out April 20th<br><br>
RELEASED ON : Roche Musique<br><br>
<br><br>
After the success of « Romance » Darius comes back with the single « Helios » featuring Wayne Snow to be released on Roche Musique. It’s with no doubt that Darius has shown his special touch, and « Helios » is another proof of the producer’s talent and singularity. The single is a deep and lovely ballad that fits perfectly to the voice of Wayne Snow. « Helios » is a catchy track that promises strong feedbacks.
