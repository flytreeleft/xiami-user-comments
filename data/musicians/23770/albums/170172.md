## 简介

<div>
<div>Suomi Power, a sentence and genre that makes many Metal Heads shake their heads. This special kind of Power has taken the world with storm and is today a serious challenger to the German Power, about the Throne of Power!</div><br>
<div>If they already beaten German or not, won't I even speculate in right now, because I'm going to focus my last lines on the new single of the band Dreamtale, that's from Finland and plays Suomi Power.</div><br>
<br><br>
<div>Never heard of them? Well, they aren't the biggest band from Finland, but they have released 2 albums and they have a new one (&amp;Difference&amp;) on their way.</div><br>
<div>But first they're giving us a 3 track Single to suck on until the album is going to be released.</div><br>
<div>The tracks are, first the Bonus of the forthcoming album, &amp;Powerplay&amp;, and 2 tracks that's going to be featured on the regular &amp;Difference&amp;, &amp;New Life&amp; and &amp;Mirror&amp;.</div><br>
<br><br>
<div>New for this releases you have the singer, Jarkko Ahola and guitarist Mikko Mattila.</div><br>
<div>And a new singer means that they has 3 different singer for their 3 albums, pretty dangerous if you ask me, peoples has a strange way to turn their backs on a band if they lose a member or two.</div><br>
<br><br>
<div>But anyway, let's look closer on the three tracks, first out is the track &amp;Powerplay&amp;, that's a pretty strange tune, it's very up-tempo and it has this &amp;dunk-dunk&amp; sound that's more suitable for Techno or something.</div><br>
<div>But in some strange way this song is really good.</div><br>
<br><br>
<div>Second we have &amp;New Life&amp;, a very usual Power Metal tune in almost mid-tempo with just faster parts. It's not that catchy, even if I think the song is very good arranged. But it's not the best.</div><br>
<br><br>
<div>Third and last we have &amp;Mirror&amp; that's going to be on the new album &amp;Difference&amp; exactly as the one before.</div><br>
<div>&amp;Mirror&amp; is the slowest and got a &amp;sad&amp; touch in the lyrics and the vocals. A very good tune, and with these three songs I'm really looking forward to hear &amp;Difference&amp;.</div><br>
<br><br>
<div>And before I end I think I should add a couple of lines about the new singer, Ahola. For all of you that heard their past albums and like the vocals, well I don't think you should worry that much, Ahola is different from Keränen and Viiltola, but he is a surprisingly good singer and got much emotions in his voice.</div><br>
<br><br>
<div>If you heard and like Dreamtale, well get the new album when it comes out, and if you can't wait, well then you have this single &amp;Wellon&amp; to quench your thirst with.</div><br>
<div>If you never heard Dreamtale but loves Finnish Power, don't hesitate, buy this single together with any of their past two albums now and find out if it's something for you.(From <a href="&amp;<a" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener">http://www.metalstorm.net/pub/review.php?review_id=856</a>"&gt;metalstorm)</div>
</div>
