## 简介

<div>
《节省钱》 is a free chinese mixtape I started as a passion project to pay homage to my favorite overseas musicians and to spread their music in the US. The first edition includes songs I listened to growing up from Leehom David Tao, and Amei - as well as more recent songs by Kimbereley Chen, Wanting Qu, JJ Lin, Jay Chou, and Nicky Lee.
