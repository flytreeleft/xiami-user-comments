## 简介

<div>
喝再多的酒，在心底还是会有一个人更让你沉醉。在那个瞬间深刻感觉到，所有的酒，都不如你。<br>
一年半的酒，那个人，一些莫名的情愫，或是刻骨的爱，或是欢愉的恋，或是易碎的梦，或是失眠的夜……这些流淌的时光，所幸让相遇并不漫长。这是鹿先森为你吟唱的歌谣，愿一切温暖又美好，愿生命中的悲欢与爱随这些音符肆意流淌。拼命期许着，醒来有晨光。<br>
Drink more wine, there still is a person in your heart to make you more intoxicated. In that moment, I felt deeply that all the wine is not as good as you.<br>
The wine, that person, some inexplicable feelings, or deeply loves, happy moments, and the fragile dream, sleepless nights... These flowing time, fortunately, let you meet me. This is the song that SirDeer sing for you, wish all be warm and beautiful. Wish joys and sorrows in life flow freely with these notes and wake up with morning light.
</div>
