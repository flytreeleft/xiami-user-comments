## 简介

<div>
Uploaded upon request. I promised myself not to remake most of my older songs because they mean a lot to me the original way they are, especially Another Wind. It's kind of like seeing old stepping stones that I've crossed to where I've come today. But what I did was take the version of this song, and mixed it so the volume is up and most of the hissing is gone. <br><br>

As the main song that brought me most of you fans today, ENJOY.
