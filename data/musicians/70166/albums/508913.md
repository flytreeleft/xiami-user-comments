## 简介

<div>
Kenichiro Nishihara的一张早期专辑<br><br>
<br><br>
<div>Electronica, ambient and experimental original soundtrack album from Kenichiro Nishihara, specially composed for fashion shows.</div><br>
<div>Genre: Electronic: Electronica</div><br>
<div>Release Date: 2003</div><br>
<br><br>
An early album from Kenichiro Nishihara, a Japanese musician and producer whose music is based in jazz, hip-hop with a crossover jazz feel.<br>
<br><br>
<div>This CD is unique among his past albums. He reconstructed songs which were played in the past fashion shows, that he worked as a music producer, for this CD.</div>
