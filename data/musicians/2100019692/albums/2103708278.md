## 简介

<div>
王嘉尔为勇闯天涯superX量身定制单曲《X》热血首发，<br><br>
这首《X》单曲不仅诠释了嘉尔一贯对于音乐的专注执着，<br><br>
更是一种挑战生活的积极态度。<br><br>
<br><br>
Jackson Wang’s custom-made single X for Snow Beer’s subsidiary brand superX, has made its anticipated debut to the Chinese public. <br><br>
This single not only reflects Jackson’s constant concentration on music, but also shows an initiative attitude when facing the challenging of life.<br><br>
<br><br>
《X》的创作过程是嘉尔从未有过的全新体验。喝下勇闯天涯superX的每一口，劲爽、畅快的感觉瞬间让王嘉尔灵感爆棚，啤酒与音符的碰撞，《X》因此而生。<br><br>
The creative process of X was an unprecedented experience to Jackson. The cool and smooth feeling of drinking superX lightened up Jackson’s mind. The liquid collides with the notes, therefore  X was born.<br><br>
<br><br>
每一瓶勇闯天涯superX中都封印着王嘉尔的创作体验，品尝superX劲爽口感，一同感受王嘉尔创作时灵感迸发的激情。<br><br>
Every bottle of superX has sealed part of Jackson’s inspiration in it. Let’s taste the cool feeling of superX, share Jackson’s brilliant creative passion together.<br>
</div>
