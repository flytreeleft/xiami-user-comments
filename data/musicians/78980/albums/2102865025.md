## 简介

<div>
<!--StartFragment --><br><br>
阔别四年，环球音乐旗下国际原创音乐人曲婉婷携全新原创大碟《LLL》回归！<br><br>
这是一次超出想象，别具一格的回归。专辑名称“LLL”意为LOVE（爱），LOSS（失），LATITUDE（自由）。邀请乐坛实力唱将John Legend，Mariah Carey，Miley Cyrus，Joss Stone幕后的金牌推手Justin Gray操刀制作。<br><br>
和以前的专辑一样，这张专辑由曲婉婷一手包办作词作曲，全程在洛杉矶录制完成。一改往日以钢琴与吉他为基调的固有模式，将国际主流EDM电子风格融入其中，彰显强劲实力与感染力。整张专辑从封面到音乐都展现了一个全新的曲婉婷。<br><br>
Wanting was signed by Nettwerk Records as their first Chinese artist in 2009. Since then, she has quickly risen in fame as one of China’s most recognized singer-songwriters – selling over a billion downloads in Asia. The Vancouver based, multi-platinum selling artist is back with her new album LLL (Love Loss Latitude). Produced by Justin Gray (John Legend, Mariah Carey, Miley Cyrus, Joss Stone) LLL releases on October 27, 2017 via Nettwerk.
</div>
