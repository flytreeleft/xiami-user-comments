## 简介

<div>
Peach Illusion 首张同名专辑「桃子假象」在七月随香香的桃子季上市。<br><br>
<br><br>
圈圈温柔而少女气的词／曲 + C.c浪漫而多变幻的编／混 = Peach Illusion与你自在起舞的夏夜&nbsp;<br><br>
<br><br>
亦真亦假，冷暖变幻的城市里，充斥着纯真而甜美的假象。<br><br>
午夜时分，摆脱了所谓“得体”的外壳，才似乎看到了朦胧而真实的世界。<br><br>
不需要让所有人理解和包容，总有人愿意站在雨中，拥抱你的凹凸不平。<br><br>
那么，在桃子假象里，你看到的又是什么？<br><br>
（特别感谢：李春银桑／设计、小吉／琪琪音像、真真／Mia Clothing Store、陈振超／友情贝斯、胡大毛／Mix Studio录音室）<br><br>
<br><br>
The eponymous debut album of Peach Illusion is released with fragrance during the peach season, July, 2018.<br><br>
<br><br>
Soft &amp; girlish Songwriting from O.o + Romantic &amp; borderless Producing from C.c = Footloose Dancing Summer Night with Peach Illusion<br><br>
<br><br>
Lingering around the edge of truth and the fluctuation of temperature, illusion with innocent peach sweetness is filling the city.<br><br>
Midnight, we dance without the shell of normal elegance, it appears to a real world, albeit blurring.<br><br>
Don’t need to be understood by everyone, as someone is standing in the rain, ready to embrace your shadow.<br><br>
Alors…what do you see in the Peach Illusion?<br><br>
Special thanks to Li Chun Yin Sang / Illustration Designer, Xiao Ji / QiiiSnacksRecords, Zhen Zhen / Mia Clothing Store, Soda Chan / Bass, Hu Damao / Mix Studio
