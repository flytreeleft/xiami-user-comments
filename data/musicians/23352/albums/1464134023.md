## 简介

<div>
加拿大小魔女Avril Lavigne经历了为人妻、离婚、又重浴爱河的人生起伏之后，继续推出了任性的全新单曲“Here’s To Never Growing Up”，不仅没有任何沧桑感觉，反而依旧是青春气息十足。单曲被乐评人评价为有很多流行单曲的影子。<br><br>
<br><br>
"Here's to Never Growing Up" is a song by Canadian recording artist Avril Lavigne, taken as the lead single from her fifth studio album. The song, written by Lavigne, David Hodges, Jacob Kasher with its producer Martin Johnson will be released on 9 April 2013. <br><br>
<br><br>
"Here's to Never Growing Up" was written by Lavigne, David Hodges, Chad Kroeger, Jacob Kasher and producer of the song Martin Johnson. Lavigne announced the title of the song on 8 February, 2013 via Twitter. On 20 March, 2013, Lavigne announced the scheduled release date for the song to be April 9, 2013, and released an 11-second snippet of the track on YouTube. Lavigne later released the single's official artwork via Twitter on April 3, 2013. The song is set to premiere on iTunes on April 9, 2013, and receive its radio premiere the same day on On Air with Ryan Seacrest. The official lyric video for "Here’s to Never Growing Up", which is entirely made up of fan submitted photos and videos, will also be released on April 9, 2013.
