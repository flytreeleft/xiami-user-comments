## 简介

<div>
Control Room is a live acoustic EP by Avril Lavigne released April 15, 2008 as a download only and is available at many online music stores. The videos for the tracks can be watched on MSN.<br><br>
<br><br>
The tracks were recorded at The Roxy Theatre in Los Angeles, California. Most of them were also included in the single for "The Best Damn Thing".<br><br>
