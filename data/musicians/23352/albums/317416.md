## 简介

<div>
<div>Live Acoustic EP is an EP that was released in 2004 by Canadian singer Avril Lavigne. It was made up of songs from her 2004 album, Under My Skin, her hit single &amp;Sk8er Boi&amp; and her 2002 album Let Go.</div><br>
<div>It is an acoustic album that was only released in United States Target stores. All the songs were recorded live during Avril's North American mall tour.</div>
