## 简介

<div>
《我有一个装满星星的口袋》是彩虹合唱团2019乐季的主题，也是金承志为所有不被理解的少数群体和我们每一个人所写的一首作品。<br><br>
我们相信每一个人都有一个属于自己的小世界，这里保留了一个个不愿意被人知道、不愿意长大的小秘密和小童心。<br><br>
愿所有内心的美好都能像星星一样，永远在属于自己的夜空中闪耀。<br><br>
<br><br>
词曲：金承志<br><br>
演唱：上海彩虹室内合唱团<br><br>
指挥：金承志<br><br>
钢琴：吴经纬<br><br>
录音/混音：莫家伟<br><br>
编曲：田汨<br><br>
<br><br>
“Pocketful of Stars” is the theme of RCS Tour in Season 2019, which is also written by Mr Jin Chengzhi, for those people sometimes cannot be understood well by others but still have strong willingness to be understood. Surely, we can also regard that the song is written for everyone. We truly believe that we all have our own zone where we place our little secrets and childlike thoughts and we wish all the goodliness in hearts can sparkle permanently like twinkling stars in the sky.
</div>
