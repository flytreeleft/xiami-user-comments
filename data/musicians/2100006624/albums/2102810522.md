## 简介

<div>
这个夏天太热，我们无心聚焦“社会问题”，无力承担“史诗巨著”，但是我真的真的真的真的好想……这只是一首主歌副歌毫无关系的无厘头作品，您在听完之后的一句笑骂，就是对我们最大的褒奖！<br><br>
<br><br>
词曲：金承志 <br><br>
指挥：金承志 <br><br>
钢琴伴奏：白苑彤 <br><br>
打击乐：俞国俊 <br><br>
演唱：上海彩虹室内合唱团 <br><br>
录音/混音：莫家伟<br><br>
封面：阿饼<br><br>
<br><br>
It was quite hot in Shanghai, 2017's summer, news like "Temperature of tomorrow will rise to a new peak of this summer" were often blown out; in the beginning of this work, RCS depicts the extremely hot weather through a chilling music with exaggerating words like "the whole street is like a pan with smog, people are rare steaks in it", "your classmate next door melted in Disneyland". However, in the middle of the song it turns out to reveal a completely different feeling: after shouting "I do want to fall in with you! ", the choir put up flower rings and dance with the Güiro as well as the Conga. The later part show eagerness for love with no more complaining, but repeatedly proclaim ALL I NEED IS LOVE! ALL! I! NEED! IS! LOVE!
</div>
