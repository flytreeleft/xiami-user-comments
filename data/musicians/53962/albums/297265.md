## 简介

<div>
Often compared to the sounds of Tribe Called Quest, The Roots, and Common, Kero One is a full time deejay, producer, rapper/songwriter, and record label owner based in San Francisco. By demand, Kero brings us the Windmills of the Soul Instrumentals album with all vocals stripped and added bonus tracks 'Space Cadets' &amp; 'Just Groovin'. The album features heavy usage of live instrumentation that Kero One plays himself such as bass guitar, Fender Rhodes keyboards, &amp; moog synthesizers, as well as saxophones and guitar from jazz/funk session players. However, not only are there jazz/funk references on Windmills, there are also references to Brazilian styles such as on 'Windmills Beats' and soul references on tracks such as 'In A Dream' which portrait some of the different styles of music that influence Kero. <br><br>
