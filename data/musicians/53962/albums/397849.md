## 简介

<div>
After Kero One's successful release of "Windmills of The Soul", which sold more than 10,000 copies with a personal credit card budget, the song that people kept talking about was "In All the Wrong Places". The track features live fender Rhodes, jazz guitar, sax, and moog by Kero One along with the helping hand of some professional jazz session players. This 12" single features an extended intro for ease of mixing and the b-side "Give Thanks" includes an exclusive Sound Providers remix. This record is a 12" that DJ's will love.
