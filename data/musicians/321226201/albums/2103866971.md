## 简介

<div>
<p> <strong>竹笛改编作品</strong>。</p><br>
<p><strong>竹笛/封面设计/混音：镜决</strong></p><br>
<p>原曲来自S.E.N.S的《春爛漫》出自专辑<span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">「源氏物语千年纪 Genji」的OST，于2009年2月25日发行。</span></p><br>
<p> </p><br>
<p><strong><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">【爛漫·夏眠之梦】</span></strong></p><br>
<p>曲子的灵感是源于初三那会儿迷恋的源氏物语。千年纪的OST中，《春爛漫》为之最，仿佛眼前便是夕颜被光源氏搂在怀中赏月的情景。印象里，光源氏心中最清纯可人的女子就是夕颜了，要不是一晚便早逝了，光源氏或许不会再心无寄托，找寻体己之人吧，或许也不会再寻若紫。</p><br>
<p> </p><br>
<p><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">在设计封面的时候，想着如何才能表现天真烂漫美好的感觉。</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">晚上读到恋爱的和歌，宜秋門院丹後的诗，便用上了：</span></p><br>
<p><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">【</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">思ひ寝の夢になぐさむ恋なれば、</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">逢はねど暮れの空ぞ待たるる。】</span></p><br>
<p> </p><br>
<p><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">现代日语翻译过来就是：</span></p><br>
<p><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">【</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">なかなか現実には会えませんが、眠れば夢で貴方に会えるので夕暮れが待たれます。】</span></p><br>
<p><span style="color: rgb(51, 63, 72); font-family: HelveticaNeue-Light, Helvetica-Light, Arial, PingFangSC-Light, " microsoft="" yahei",="" 微软雅黑,="" sans-serif;="" letter-spacing:="" 1px;"="">虽然很难见到现实，但是如果能睡的话就会在梦中和你见面，所以会等待黄昏。</span></p><br>
<p> </p><br>
